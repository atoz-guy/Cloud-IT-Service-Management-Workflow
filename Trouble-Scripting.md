**📋 Standardized Troubleshooting Script**

When taking ownership of a new ticket, use the following questions to gather necessary diagnostic data.
Professional documentation relies on consistent communication.

**1. Discovery Questions**

**Visual Verification:**
**"Can you describe exactly what you see on your screen when the error occurs? If possible, please attach a screenshot."**

**Timeline:**
**"When did this issue first start happening?"**

**Standard Procedures:**
**"Have you tried restarting your machine or the specific application yet?"**

**Scope of Impact:**
**"Is anyone else on your team experiencing this same problem?"**

**Change Management:**
**"Did you make any recent changes, such as installing new software, moving files, or connecting new hardware?"**
