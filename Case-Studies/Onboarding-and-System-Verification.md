# Case Study: Spiceworks Cloud Help Desk Initialization

## Overview
As part of establishing a Cloud-IT Service Management (ITSM) infrastructure for my home lab, I performed the initial configuration and verification of the Spiceworks Cloud Help Desk instance.

## Incident Details
* **Category:** System Administration and new client Onboarding
* **Status:** Resolved
* **Objective:**  Was to Ensure the ticketing intake system is operational and completely audit-ready.

## Actions Taken
1. **System Audit:** Reviewed initial automated "Welcome" and "Import" tickets generated by the platform when account was created.
2. **Ownership:** Assigned the tickets to the primary technician account (Afiz Jiwa) to demonstrate ownership and accountability.
3. **Resolution:** I Verified that the ticketing queue was functional and documented the closure of onboarding tasks to maintain a clean, professional audit trail.

## Lessons Learned
* **Importance of Documentation:** All forms of automated/system-generated tickets should be tracked and closed rather than deleted, data and information is the key. Ensuring a complete record of system activity.
* **Audit Trails:** In the end I maintained a clean "Open" queue as is essential for effective ticket management and timely SLA (service Level Agreement) response.
